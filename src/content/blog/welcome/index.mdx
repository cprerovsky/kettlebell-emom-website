We believe in:

**Longevity over intensity** - Training you can sustain at 50 is more valuable than training that destroys you at 25

**Simplicity over complexity** - If you need a spreadsheet to train, you'll eventually stop training

**Function over aesthetics** - Strong enough to live well, not strong enough to impress strangers

## The Kettlebell EMOM Approach

The **Every Minute On the Minute (EMOM)** format is the core of our training philosophy:

1. **Every minute, a timer beeps**
2. **You perform your exercise for 30-40 seconds**
3. **You rest for the remainder of the minute**
4. **Repeat for 15-20 minutes**

That's it. No complicated programming. No analysis paralysis. Just structured work with built-in rest.

### Why This Works

**It removes decisions** - The timer tells you when to work and when to rest

**It enforces consistency** - 20 minutes, 3 times per week is sustainable indefinitely

**It's measurable** - Did you complete the workout? Yes or no. That's your metric.

**It scales** - Beginners and advanced trainees use the same format with different weights
